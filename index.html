Email: gaomingppm [at] njupt.edu.cn

Ming Gao is currently a Professor of the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications. He obtained the Ph.D. degree from the school of cyber science and technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU), advised by Prof. Jinsong Han and co-advised by Prof. Feng Lin.

His research interests include Mobile Sensing, Pervasive Computing, IoT Security and Privacy Protection.
